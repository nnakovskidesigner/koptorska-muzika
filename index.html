play [track name or URL] - the bot will automatically join the user's current voice channel and play the provided track as an argument. YouTube Music and Spotify URLs work too as well as regular YouTube links. If the provided query is a name, the bot will by default search for the track (video) on YouTube. Playlist links work too and the bot will put the tracks in a queue which will be played in their respective order.
